About Jason

Bio

Jason Sykes was born and raised in Bartlett, TN. At a very young age Jason began serving his community in numerous volunteer capacities.

A graduate of Bartlett High School, Jason soon began work with the Youth Villages corporation. There he developed a passion for serving others and helping those less fortunate to achieve their goals.

Later, Jason served with the nonprofit organization, Lifeblood, helping save lives in the community by organizing countless blood drives and helping the organization grow in influence and capacity.

He was appointed by the Mayor to the Beautification Committee in 2009 where he still serves today.

Beliefs

Jason is committed to strong, conservative, family values. He is passionate about our God-given responsibility to serve one another, a standard he lives every day.

Jason believes that the greatness of a community is directly connected to its goodness, and Jason is committed to presenting his community opportunities to show goodness of character, compassion, and stewardship.

Education is primary concern for any responsible parent, and Jason's son is going to be joining the public school system soon. This makes the issues of the Public School system in Bartlett a key concern, one to which he is personally invested.

Jason is convinced that the more communication and interaction a community can have, the closer that community's people will become. As a result, they will be more in tune with the needs, goals, and accomplishments of that community.

Jason is determined to work to increase this community interaction by sponsering as many family-friendly community events as possible.

Priorities

Bartlett is a town on the move. There is progress in every area, and the people of Bartlett are uniting with a new sense of purpose. From job growth to neighborhood revitalization, from downtown improvements to dramatic reductions in crime, Bartlett is only getting better.

Great cities have great leadership. That's why Jason Sykes is the best choice for Alderman. His energy and passion are a custom fit for the fast pace to greatness that Bartlett has shown. His vision for a strong foundation in family values and wise stewardship makes him ideally suited to serve this community.

What is an Alderman?

An alderman is a member of a municipal assembly or council in many jurisdictions founded upon English law. The term maybe titular, denoting a high-ranking member of a borough or county council, a council member chosen by the elected members themselves rather than by popular vote, or a council member elected by voters.

The title is derived from the Old English title of ealdorman, literally meaning "elder man", and was used by the chief nobles presiding over shires.

"Board of Aldermen" is the governing executive or legislative body of many cities and towns in the United States. The term is sometimes used instead of city council, or is used of an executive board independent of the council, or is used of what amounts to an upper house of a bicameral legislature. Boards of Aldermen are used in many rural areas of the United States as opposed to a larger city council or city commission.
